## Relevant Context 2
If you have many items, keep them in your shopping cart until you are ready to scan. 1
Start the Checkout Process: Approach the self-checkout machine and look for a Start button on the screen. Click it to begin the checkout process. 1
Scan Your Items: Use the barcode scanner to scan each item. Ensure that the products have barcodes; if not, you may need to look them up using the machine's interface or enter a PLU code for produce. 2
Bag Your Items: After scanning, place your items in the bagging area. The machine may have weight sensors to confirm that the item you scanned matches the weight of the item placed in the bag. 1
Handle Age-Restricted Items: If you scan an age-restricted item (like alcohol), the machine will lock the screen until a staff member verifies your age.
